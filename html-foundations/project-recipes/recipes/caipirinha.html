Caipirinha

Description

The Caipirinha is Brazil's most famous and refreshing cocktail, a perfect balance of strong, sweet, and sour. Made with just three ingredients—cachaça, lime, and sugar—it's a vibrant and potent drink that embodies the lively spirit of Brazilian culture.

Ingredients

  • 1 large lime, washed well
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 oz (60 ml) cachaça
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Cut the lime into small wedges, removing the central white pith if desired to reduce bitterness.
  2. Place the lime wedges and sugar in a sturdy old-fashioned glass.
  3. Muddle firmly to crush the lime and release all its juice.
  4. Fill the glass with ice cubes. Pour the cachaça over the ice, stir well to combine everything, and enjoy immediately.
