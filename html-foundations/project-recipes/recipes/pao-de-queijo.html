Pão de Queijo

Description

Pão de Queijo is a gluten-free Brazilian cheese bread known for its unique chewy texture and addictive cheesy flavor. Made with tapioca flour, these puffs are golden on the outside, soft and airy on the inside, and perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese (or traditional Minas cheese)
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, bring the milk, oil, and salt to a boil.
  3. Place the tapioca flour in a large bowl and immediately pour the hot milk mixture over it.
  4. Stir until a grainy dough forms.
  5. Let it cool slightly, then mix in the grated cheese and eggs until smooth and sticky.
  6. Roll the dough into golf ball-sized portions and place them on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes until puffed and golden.
